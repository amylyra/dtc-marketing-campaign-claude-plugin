## 1. Campaign Overview

**Campaign name:** "Gear Up Before It Freezes" — Tern Ski Season 2026 Launch

**Summary:** Two-phase campaign launching Tern's full product line for ski season, peaking at BFCM with bundle-focused offers. Phase 1 (Nov 1-26) drives awareness and early full-price sales. Phase 2 (Nov 27 - Dec 1) is the BFCM tentpole with the deepest offers of the year.

**Primary objective:** $45K in revenue across Nov 1 - Dec 15, with 60%+ at full price.

**Secondary objectives:**
- Grow email list by 2,000 subscribers (pre-BFCM capture)
- Generate 50+ pieces of UGC from field-testing customers
- Establish The Bottle Parka as the category-defining product for gear insulation

**Promo economics:**
- Phase 1 (launch): No discount. Early access for email list (48 hours). Free shipping at $60. Full margin.
- Phase 2 (BFCM): The Full Send bundle at $108 (saves $24, ~17% discount on bundle only). Free shipping sitewide during BFCM weekend. Individual SKU pricing unchanged.
- Expected margin impact: BFCM discount applies only to bundles. At projected 40% bundle attach rate, blended discount penetration stays under 20%.
- Kill threshold: If BFCM contribution margin per order drops below $18 by midday Friday, revert to free-shipping-only and pull bundle discount.

## 2. Target Audience

**Primary:** Serious winter athletes 25-45 who already own premium gear and have experienced frozen water bottles, dead phone batteries, and fogged goggles firsthand. They discover brands through Instagram outdoor communities, Reddit (r/skiing, r/mountaineering), YouTube gear reviews, and word-of-mouth at trailheads.

> "Men and women 25-45 who care about gear performance and spend on quality equipment, shopping for winter sport accessories because their existing gear fails in extreme cold. They discover brands through outdoor creator content and gear review communities, and are influenced by field-test data and peer recommendations."

**Secondary:** Gift-givers (partners, parents, friends) shopping for "the skier who has everything." Peak activity: Nov 15 - Dec 20. They search "gifts for skiers" and "gifts for outdoor lovers."

**Excluded:** Casual/resort-only skiers who rarely encounter extreme conditions. Discount-motivated bargain hunters — they degrade cohort LTV and the brand doesn't compete on price.

## 3. Key Messages

**Messaging hierarchy:**

1. **Why should I care?** Your gear freezes before you do. Frozen water bottles, dead phone batteries, fogged goggles — all solvable problems.
2. **What is it?** Tern makes insulation for the gear you already own. Purpose-built. Expedition-grade.
3. **Why Tern?** Field-tested at 14,000ft. Military-grade Thinsulate. Waterproof YKK zippers. Not an insulated bottle — insulation for YOUR bottle.
4. **What should I do?** Shop the collection. Or start with The Bottle Parka, $34.

**Supporting messages:**
- For athletes: "Tested in the conditions you actually ski/climb/hike in. Not a lab. The mountain."
- For gift-givers: "The gift they didn't know they needed — and the one they'll actually use."
- For BFCM: "The Full Send: every piece of gear protection in one kit. $108 (saves $24). Full system, one box."

**Proof points:** 4.9 stars / 2,800+ reviews on Bottle Parka. "Survived a week on Denali." Tested at 14,000ft. Military-grade materials.

## 10. Execution Checklist

### Before Launch

1. **Objective?** $45K revenue, $18K+ contribution margin across Nov 1 - Dec 15.
2. **Target audience?** Serious winter athletes 25-45. Excluded: casual skiers, bargain hunters.
3. **Behavior we want?** Phase 1: full-price purchase or email signup. Phase 2: bundle purchase during BFCM.
4. **Offer?** Phase 1: early access (no discount), free shipping at $60. Phase 2: Full Send bundle at $108 (saves $24), free shipping sitewide.
5. **Why not a cheaper incentive?** Phase 1 IS the cheapest (early access = zero cost). Phase 2 uses bundle savings (obscures per-unit discount, protects individual pricing).
6. **Margin impact?** Bundle discount is ~17% on Full Send only. At 40% bundle attach, blended discount penetration is ~7% of total revenue. Well within 15-25% guardrail.
7. **Products included/excluded?** All 4 SKUs + 2 bundles included. Individual SKU prices never discounted. Hero (Bottle Parka) price stays at $34.
8. **Stacking?** No. BFCM bundle price does not stack with any other codes.
9. **Inventory?** 20% buffer on Bottle Parka. All SKUs stocked for projected volume.
10. **Kill threshold?** Contribution margin/order <$18 by midday Black Friday → pull bundle discount, revert to free shipping only.
